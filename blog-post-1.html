Automatic Exploratory Data Analytics

Imagine your friend gave you free passes for Ed Sheeren concert which is in US. You are all excieted to go but it is your first time in US. What you will do? Will you plan your trip and research a bit about location, travel, food and stay or you will just jump on opportunity and find yourself puzzled with lots of questions which needs to be answered in order to make a decision.

So here your decision is based on lots of things like date of concert, availability of tickets, taking leaves from your office and taking care of so many other dependancies. In order to take decision (to go or not) first you will do analyis in each aspect and see what are your dependencies and factors imapcting your decision. This common phenomeon is EDA ( exploratory data analysis) in the language of Data Scientist.

Exploratory Data Analysis refers to the critical process of performing initial investigations on data so as to discover patterns,to spot anomalies,to test hypothesis and to check assumptions with the help of summary statistics and graphical representations.
Larry L. Johnson

It is important to understand your data before applying any ML/DL/Statastics on it. In data analyis first you check quality of the data which can be understood by knowing fill rate, missing or null values, duplicates, and data types of variables. The relationship of target variable with all other variables is important to know any bias in the data. It is also important to statisfy the assumptions of different algorithms.

Pandas becomes obivious choice for all data analyis tasks due to availabilibity of numerous features which are easy to apply. But whenever you get any new data from the client you have to run some basic set of commands on jupyter notebook or other editor. In order to stop this repeatation and to save time for Data Scientists.

I am introducing one package primary_data_analyis which gives you all information about dataset with just one command. You can get all the data quality information in therms of colorful tables and data interactions can be achieved with interactive Plotly graphs. All the plots and final html report document is saved in your desired folder which you can access anytime. The package covers all important data analyis part which is recommednded whenever you gets any new data. In the output you get colorful, interactive, well documented html file which can be use to develope your understanding about data.
